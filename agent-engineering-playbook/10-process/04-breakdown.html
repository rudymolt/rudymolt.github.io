Vertical Slice Checker

One Feature, Cut Into Three Slices

The booking form from the alignment page, after breakdown. Each slice is a thin wedge through every layer, and each one is on its own:

Slice 1

User can see free slots.

A calendar screen, the logic that finds free slots, the table they live in, and a test. Depends on nothing.

Slice 2

User can book a slot.

The booking button, the double-booking check, the saved booking, and a test. Depends on #1.

Slice 3

User gets a confirmation email.

The email template, the send-on-booking logic, and a test. Depends on #2.
